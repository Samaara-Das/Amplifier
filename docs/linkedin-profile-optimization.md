## Action Map — the 6 moves, distilled

Each point is what we actually do on Sammy's profile. The raw transcript is preserved below.

### 1. Banner (2400 × 600 px)
- Treat as prime real estate.
- Say **what you do** + **the problem you solve** + a **clear CTA** (where to buy / where to contact).
- Simple, uncluttered design.
- Creator's example (fitness): bad = "personalized workouts, meal prep, 1-on-1 coaching, contact me." Good = "I help busy entrepreneurs lose fat and keep it off in 90 days — sign up below."
- **For Sammy (draft direction):** "I help working professionals build a second income stream from the US markets — without quitting their job. Free indicators below."

### 2. Featured section — 3-tier offer ladder
Most underused section on LinkedIn. Don't pin a viral post or a random link.
- **Low-ticket (<$100):** newsletter signup, free PDF, free indicator.
- **Mid-ticket:** 1-on-1 consulting, $X per hour.
- **High-ticket:** application to retainer / monthly program (>$1K).
- Treat each thumbnail like a YouTube thumbnail — clear, curiosity-driving, entices click.
- **For Sammy (draft direction):** Low = free TradingView indicators (already published). Mid = ? (defer — no offer yet). High = $20/month premium indicators (this is the funnel endpoint, not high-ticket in classical sense — Sammy's model is volume, not consulting).

### 3. Content — "better than anyone's paid content" is the bare minimum
LinkedIn is saturated. Stand out by sharing:
- Specific stories
- Your systems
- How-tos (build authority + trust)
- Testimonials / social proof
- Storytelling posts
- **For Sammy:** maps directly to the 5 content pillars (Stop Losing Money, Make Money While You Sleep, Market Cheat Code, Proof Not Promises, Future-Proof Your Income). LinkedIn-specific angle = career security + AI fear + side income for professionals.

### 4. Profile link (the hidden CTA)
LinkedIn has a built-in "add a link under your name" feature. Most people miss it.
- Point it at a funnel / landing page / lead magnet.
- Make it stupid-easy to hand over an email or buy.
- **For Sammy (draft direction):** link to a simple landing page with free TradingView indicators (email capture) → $20/mo upsell in follow-up sequence.

### 5. Social selling via comments
Creator's claim: made $10K passively with this one move.
- Post educational / storytelling content — NO sell in the post.
- Drop the CTA as the FIRST comment on your own post.
- LinkedIn surfaces the author's own first comment at the top of the comment thread.
- Post stays clean + valuable. Comment = soft conversion handoff.
- Creator's CTA template: "You can buy my LinkedIn Playbook here → [link]"
- **For Sammy (draft direction):** post = pure value. First comment = "If you want the free indicator I used to spot this setup, it's pinned in my Featured → [link]"

### 6. Headline — What / Who / How formula
Weak: "CEO of X" / "Founder of Y" — tells who you are, not how you help.
Strong formula:
- **What** do you do?
- **Who** do you do it for?
- **How** do you do it?
- Ask: "what is the PROMISE I'm making to the ideal person viewing my profile?"
- Headline + profile photo are the ONLY things shown consistently in the feed — treat them as the storefront.
- **For Sammy (draft direction):** "Helping working professionals build a second income from the US markets | Free TradingView indicators + strategies that show their work" — needs tightening. We'll iterate.

### Bonus — profile photo
Creator's drive-by note: "you should really update your profile photo." Headline + photo are what the feed shows. If either is weak, nothing else matters.

## Execution order (for Sammy's profile)

1. **Headline** — single highest-leverage change. Do first.
2. **Profile photo** — audit current one. Upgrade if needed.
3. **Banner** — design once headline is locked (banner should reinforce headline).
4. **Profile link under name** — point at free-indicator landing page (may need to build page).
5. **About / Summary** — not covered in transcript but mandatory. Emotion-first hook → proof → CTA.

7. **Experience** — reframe around brand promise, not resume.
8. **First post (reactivation)** — wake the 9,500 dormant connections. Separate deliverable.
