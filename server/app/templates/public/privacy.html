Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-30

This Privacy Policy explains how Amplifier ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use our platform at api.pointcapitalis.com. The Service is intended for US-based users only.

  1. What We Collect. We collect the following categories of information:
    • Account information: email address and hashed password.
    • Profile information: social media platform usernames and connected account details you provide during onboarding.
    • Audience data: follower counts, niche tags, and audience region — used for campaign matching.
    • Content data: URLs of posts you submit through the platform and the content text associated with those posts.
    • Engagement metrics: likes, comments, shares, and impressions scraped from your public posts to calculate earnings.
    • Payment information: Stripe Connect account ID for users who request payouts. We do not store full bank account or card numbers.
    • Usage data: timestamps of logins, API calls, and platform actions logged for security and anti-fraud purposes.
  2. How We Use Your Information. We use the information we collect to:
    • Match you to campaigns that fit your audience and niche.
    • Calculate and credit your earnings based on verified post performance.
    • Process payout requests through Stripe Connect.
    • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity, including fake metrics and coordinated inauthentic behavior.
    • Communicate with you about your account, earnings, and platform updates.
  3. What We Share. We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or third parties. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:
    • Stripe: We share your Stripe account ID and payout amounts with Stripe to process withdrawals. Stripe's own privacy policy governs their handling of this data.
    • Advertisers: We share aggregated, non-identifying campaign performance metrics with the companies who fund campaigns. We do not share your email address or personal profile with advertisers.
    • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Amplifier or its users.
  4. Data Retention. We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 90 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance purposes (such as payout records).
  5. US-Only Audience. The Service is operated from the United States and is intended solely for US residents. We do not knowingly collect data from users outside the United States. If you are accessing the Service from outside the US, you do so at your own risk and the Service makes no representations about compliance with non-US data protection laws.
  6. Security. We use AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive stored credentials and HTTPS for all data in transit. We employ rate limiting, anomaly detection, and audit logging to protect against unauthorized access. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
  7. Your Rights and Contact. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at support@pointcapitalis.com. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days. We may update this Privacy Policy at any time by posting the revised version to this page with an updated date.
